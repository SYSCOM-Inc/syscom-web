Why SYSCOM

Four decades of solving hard problems

We are not a body shop. We build and own intellectual property. We know that the newest technology is not always the best solution — but it is sometimes the only solution, and we know the difference.

40+ Years of Continuous Operation

Founded in 1982 in Baltimore, SYSCOM has been solving enterprise content challenges since before the term "ECM" existed. That kind of longevity in this industry is extremely rare — and it means we have seen every architecture, every migration path, and every failure mode.

AI-Forward, Legacy-Deep

Our CTO leads a dedicated AI strategy that brings zero-shot classification, semantic search, and intelligent automation to every product. But we never apply AI for its own sake — we apply it where it actually solves the problem better than traditional approaches.

Government & Financial Focus

State agencies, federal departments, credit unions, banks, and insurance carriers trust SYSCOM with their most sensitive documents. We understand compliance requirements — NIST 800-53, CJIS, IRS Pub 1075, HIPAA, SOX — because our clients live under them every day.

We Own Our IP
